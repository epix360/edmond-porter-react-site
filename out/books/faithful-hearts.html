Faithful Hearts

Featuring Edmond A Porter

Heartwarming stories and poems celebrating our furriest family members: our pets.

Buy on Amazonopen_in_new

Our relationships with our pets predate civilization itself. And why not? Animals have an amazing capacity to teach us about life and love. Be they fur babies, best friends, or just plain family, pets shine in Faithful Hearts. The true (or based-on-true) essays and poems in this collection range from funny to sweet to just a bit salty. They feature dogs, cats, horses, and more. Pet lovers are sure to find a story or two that speaks to them.
